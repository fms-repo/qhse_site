Training Management Module

Core Module Mobile Enabled AI-Powered

Manage all safety training and competency requirements across your organization. Schedule classroom and online training, track certifications, deliver e-learning courses, and ensure all personnel maintain required competencies with automated renewal reminders and gap analysis.

Complete Training Lifecycle

Manage the entire training lifecycle from requirement identification to certification tracking. Schedule classroom training sessions, deliver online courses, track attendance, and manage assessments. Integration with Fit to Work ensures competency requirements are verified before work assignment.

Automated renewal reminders notify personnel when certifications are expiring. Training gap analysis identifies employees who need specific training. Mobile access enables field workers to complete online training and view their training records. Comprehensive reporting tracks training completion rates and compliance status.
