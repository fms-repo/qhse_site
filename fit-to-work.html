Fit to Work Module

Core Module Mobile Enabled Compliance

Ensure all personnel are fit for work before they begin their duties. Our comprehensive fit-to-work system manages medical assessments, drug and alcohol testing, competency checks, and certification tracking. Automated reminders ensure assessments are completed on time and certifications remain current, protecting both employee health and workplace safety.

Comprehensive Fitness Assessments

Conduct thorough fitness-for-work assessments including medical evaluations, drug and alcohol testing, and competency checks. Schedule assessments based on job requirements, regulatory mandates, or company policies. Track assessment results, medical clearances, and restrictions in a secure, centralized system.

Manage certifications and licenses with automated expiration tracking and renewal reminders. Ensure personnel certifications are current before they are assigned to work. Integration with access control systems can prevent unauthorized access to work areas when fitness assessments are not current.
